International Baccalaureate

About :

This program was founded in 1968. It was made for kids to get a better education. It was for kids through the age 3-19 years old. This program allows for many universities to recognize you.

Benefits of IB :

  1. Will allow you to become more culturally aware and you will be able to learn a second language of your choice.
  2. Will teach you how to work independently.
  3. More universities will recognize you.
  4. You will be able to meet people that come from all over the world.
